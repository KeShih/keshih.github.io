Number of edges in acyclic flow


The graph in this post are simple graphs with unit edge capacity.

Theorem1Main Theorem

Let G=(V,E)G=(V,E)G=(V,E) be a graph with nnn vertices and mmm edges, then an acyclic integer flow of value vvv saturates O(nv)O(n\sqrt{v})O(nv​) edges.

Let ddd to be the distance of the shortest path between sss and ttt. Let vvv to be the value of a sss-ttt-flow, then v≤2(n/d)2v\leq 2(n/d)^2v≤2(n/d)2 and v≤2m/dv\leq 2 m/dv≤2m/d.

ProofMain theorem
