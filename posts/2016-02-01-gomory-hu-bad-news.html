No nice generalization of Gomory-Hu tree


Gomory-Hu tree of a graph GGG is a weighted graph HHH on the same set of vertices. It has the two nice properties.

  1. It is a tree.
  2. For every s,ts,ts,t, there exist a min-ststst-cut in HHH that is also a min-ststst-cut in GGG.

We could ask for a generalization. Given a graph GGG, can we find some nice graph HHH such that

For every S,TS,TS,T such that 1≤∣S∣,∣T∣≤k1\leq |S|,|T|\leq k1≤∣S∣,∣T∣≤k and S∩T=∅S\cap T=\emptysetS∩T=∅, there is a min-STSTST-cut in HHH that is a min-STSTST-cut in GGG.
